Who are we?

CM

CM is a fan-made recreation of "F-Stop", a videogame concept created by Valve employees during the experimentation stage of Portal 2. CM's goal is to keep FStop's gameplay style whilst expanding upon through a unique art style.

It's main mechanic is a camera which you use to take photos of objects which you can then scale as you place them back into the world.
